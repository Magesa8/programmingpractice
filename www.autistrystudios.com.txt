                        <h2>Helping ASD youth become independent adults</h2>
                    </center>
                    <span class="style1">
                        <div class="entry">
                        <center>
                            
                            <em>We work with individuals 13 and older with a wide range of diagnoses<br />
                            We are located in Marin County, California</em>
                         </center>
                        </div>
                    </span>
                </div>
                <!-- Top level article post area -->
                <div class="post" id="post-2693">
                    <div class="style1">
                        <p>
                        <strong>Autistry Studios</strong> is a pre-vocational program that promotes the growth of independence. 
                        The transition from youth to adult is a difficult time for everyone. For students with Autism, 
                        Asperger’s or other similar challenges the transition to independent adulthood can be especially difficult.
                        </p> 
                        <p>
                        We work in small groups pursuing projects the students have chosen. In the context of working on these projects 
                        we learn about the individual and support constructive growth. 
                        </p>
                        <p>
                        Autistry Studios was founded by two parents who felt there was nothing else available to support their 
                        son as he set out to discover adulthood.
                        </p>

                    <h6>
                        Pre-Vocational Workshops</h6>
                    
                    Build Stuff College Support Workshops prepare for the
                    workplace and/or higher education by strengthening executive functioning and social
                    skills.
                    <br />
                    <br />
                    <h6>
                        Counseling</h6>
                    Individual, group, and family counseling is available to support our students as
                    they navigate the difficult issues of launching, self-discovery, and new relationships.
                    We also offer groups for families and siblings.
                    <br />
                    <br />
                    <h6>
                        LifeLong Learning Support</h6>
                    Our College Support Group provides both academic and social support. We offer homework
                    review as well as time management, study and organizational skill instruction. Individual
                    tutoring is available in several subject areas including Math, Science, Computer
                    Programming, History, and English.
                    <br />
                    <br />
                    We also provide a safe place to work, hang out and meet new friends.<br />
